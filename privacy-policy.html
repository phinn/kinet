Kinet Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Kinet is an offline-first fitness application. All your data is stored locally on your device. We collect the following information solely to provide app functionality:

  • Body Data: Height, weight, age, and gender — used to personalize training plans and statistics.
  • Training Records: Workout completion data, check-in history, and exercise details.
  • Weight History: Weight log entries over time for trend analysis.
  • Profile Photo: Optional avatar image stored only on your device.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized training plans and exercise recommendations.
  • Track workout progress and display training statistics.
  • Analyze weight trends and provide fitness insights.
  • Power AI-powered features (diet tips, weekly reports, coaching) — your data is sent to your self-configured AI provider only when you use these features.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. No data is sent to our servers. When AI features are used, data is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you configure (e.g., OpenAI, DeepSeek). We recommend reviewing your AI provider's privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Kinet integrates with the following third-party services:

  • AI Providers: OpenAI, DeepSeek, or other OpenAI-compatible APIs — used only when you explicitly configure and use AI features. Your data is subject to the AI provider's privacy policy.
  • Bilibili / YouTube: Video search and playback via official web interfaces. No personal data is shared with these platforms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information. Your data is never shared with third parties except:

  • When you explicitly use AI features, your fitness data is sent to your chosen AI provider.
  • When required by law or legal process.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time.
  • Correction: Edit your profile, weight logs, and training plans anytime.
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all data permanently from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Kinet is not intended for children under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes. Major changes will be notified within the app.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's Settings page.
